## Deck lifecycle (status)

`draft` → AI plan → `planned` → `api.ai.generateSlides` → `generated` / `edited` → (optional) `exported`.

- **Plan gate**: `/decks/[id]/edit` and `/decks/[id]/print` redirect to `/decks/[id]/plan` while status is `draft` or `planned`.
- **New deck**: `afterCreate: "plan"` sets status `planned` and sends user to plan; `"dashboard"` leaves `draft`.
