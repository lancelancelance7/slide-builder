# Brand kits list

## Route

- **URL**: `/brand-kits`
- **Page**: `src/app/(workspace)/brand-kits/page.tsx`
- **Client**: `BrandKitsPageClient` — `src/components/brand-kit/brand-kits-page-client.tsx`

## Role

- Prefetches **`api.brandKit.list`** on server; client **`api.brandKit.list.useQuery`**.
- Grid of kits with deck counts, accent swatch, default badge; links to editor and create.
